How to Join

01

Install Legacy CS:GO

Use the legacy branch of CS:GO (before the CS2 update) or a compatible client. Make sure VAC is enabled and your game is up to date.

02

Connect to the Bot

Add our matchmaking bot on Steam and send !queue to join the matchmaking queue. The bot will notify you when a match is found.

03

Accept & Play

Once all players accept the match, connect to the provided server IP. Play competitively and earn ELO based on your result and performance.
